2024 Annual Dinner / Inauguration of Hon. Nurudeen Olaleye as Patron at Cityfolks Club of Nigeria

About event

It's with great honor as we delight in the spirit for our annual dinner at Cityfolks, while we spontaneously inaugurate Hon. Nurudeen Olaleye (The Alawo Erin of Egbaland) as a patron at Cityfolks Club of Nigeria.
