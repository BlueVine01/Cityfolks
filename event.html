It's with great honor as we delight in the spirit for our annual dinner at Cityfolks, while we spontaneously inaugurate Hon. Nurudeen Olaleye (The Alawo Erin of Egbaland) as a patron at Cityfolks Club of Nigeria.

Government Monitoring

In today's world, It's important than ever for governments to be transparent and accountable to the people they serve. The CityFolks club of NIgeria believes that government monitoring is a vital part of healthy democracy. We aim to provide accurate and timely information on government activities to ensure that the public is informed and able to hold thier leaders accountable. We also strive to encourage dialogue and debate on important issues facing our nation, and we believe that open communication is essential for bilding a strong and vibrant society.

Workshop and Training

The Cityfolks Club of Nigeria is committed to providing workshops and training opportunities for the people of our community. Our goal is to help everyone reach their full potential, and we believe that access to high-quality workshops and training is key to achieving this goal From business development to personal growth, we offer a wide range of workshops and training opportunities to suit everyone's needs. hether you're looking to start a new business, improve your skils, or simply learn something new, we have workshops and training programs.

Educational Support

We recognize the importance of educational support for all individuals, regardless of age or background. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality education, and we are committed to helping make that happen. Through our educational support programs, we offer tutoring, mentoring and other forms of assistance to students of all ages. We also work with local schools and organizations to provide supportfor teachersand other educational professionals. Our goal is to help create a strong educational syste, that prepares students for success in life. By working together, we can ensure that no one is left behind.

Annual Dinner and Awards

Each year, the Cityfolks Club of Nigeria hosts an annual dinner and awards ceremony to celebrate the accomplishments of individuals and organizations in our community. This event is an opportunity to recognize the hard work and dedication of those who have made a positive impact on our city. We recognize the achievements of those who have helped make our community a better place to live.
