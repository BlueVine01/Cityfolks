We are a dynamic organization with a multifaceted approach to achieving its goals. By prioritizing friendship, support, community outreach, education, fundraising, and effective public relations.

our mission

To make a positive impact on the lives of members and our fellow citizens by promoting friendship, kindness and trust; and be socially responsible to our country.

our vision

A society where every member feels valued and supported, and where communities are united in working towards the betterment of all. We strive to be a catalyst for social change and humanitarian efforts, creating a more compassionate and inclusive environment for all.

our value!

The Cityfolks club of Nigeria is a community of people who share a common passion for improving their lives and the lives of those around them. We believe that by working together, we can create a better future for everyone in our city. Our values are simple but powerful: respect, integrity, and service. We respect eachother and treat everyone with kindness and compassion. We act with integrity in everything we do, always doing what is right and just. And we are committed to serving our community, using our skills and talents to make a difference. These values guide everything we do, and we invite you to join us in making our city a better place for all.
