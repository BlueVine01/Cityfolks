cityfolks club of nigeria

CityFolks is a support and soc (domestic and international), which provides opportunities for everyone from different backgrounds and nationalities to come together. We host multiple events throughout the year which allows the club members to make meaningful connections at demo.

Students, faculty and staff are all welcome to learn about different cultures while promoting respect and understanding. Diverse activities are offered all year, including International Education Week, participation in the regional Night of All Nations, and the Diversity Festival, to name a few.
