cityfolks club of nigeria

Cityfolks Club of Nigeria is a dynamic organization with a multifaceted approach to achieving its goals. By prioritizing friendship, support, community outreach, education, fundraising, and effective public relations, the club strives to make a meaningful impact on the lives of its members and the broader society.

The organization is committed to moral and financial assistance for its members, outreach to the needy in the community, educational support through scholarships, fundraising initiatives, and establishing strong relationships with the public.

history

The I-Club is one of the oldest existing clubs at TMCC and is comprised of students of all nationalities and backgrounds. While the I-Club was established as a social support network for international students, it has evolved into a social club with equal membership of international and domestic students. The I-Club provides an environment in which everyone learns about different cultures while promoting respect, understanding, and cultural consciousness.

Club activities

CityFolks club of Nigeria is committed to fostering a sense of community through the maintenance of a dedicated Club House. The Social Secretary plays a pivotal role in ensuring the smooth day-to-day functioning of the facility, with regular reporting on activities and financial matters. Our unique meeting setup at the Club House encourages members to bring their own meals, creating a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere for all.
